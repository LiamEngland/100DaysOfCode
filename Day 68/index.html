HTML5 Semantic Elements

Semantics is the study of the meanings of words and phrases in a language.

A semantic element clearly describes its meaning to both the browser and the developer.

Examples of Non-Semantic elements: div and span, as they tell nothing about the content.

Examples of Semantic elements: form, table and article, as they clearly define the content.

Semantic Elements are supported on all modern browsers.
